About Me

Specialising in Neuro-Technical Systems Synthesis. For three decades I have operated across broadcast audio, systems engineering, business leadership, and now deep into the science of how we perceive the world.

My career never changed direction — it expanded outward. Starting in live sound and studio work, moving into managing operations, running companies, and now combining that hands-on experience with formal studies in neuroscience, cognitive psychology, and behavioural economics.

Philosophy: “Technology solves problems. Biology explains why solutions feel right. Creative sensory stimulation bridges the gap.”
