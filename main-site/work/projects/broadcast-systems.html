About This Work

With many years designing, building and maintaining broadcast audio systems for outside broadcasts, live sport and major events. Work covers RF coordination, frequency planning, Dante networking, MADI routing, and full system testing.

Goal: Zero‑failure operation under pressure, delivering consistent high‑quality audio for millions of viewers. Credits include UEFA Euros, NFL London, Open Golf, Olympics and more.

Capabilities

  • RF planning & coordination — Shure, Sennheiser, Wisycom
  • Dante / AES67 networking, MADI, signal routing
  • Full system integration & compliance testing
